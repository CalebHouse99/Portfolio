About Me

Motivated and focused, I set out on my journey as a front-end developer in 2020. Since then, I've been hooked on how coding can dramatically change the way users interact with a website. My curiosity didn't stop there; I dived into back-end concepts to understand how the whole web application works, making me a well-rounded developer.

My knack for solving problems and my never-quit attitude helped me push through the tough parts, and as a result, I've gotten efficient with building user-friendly and smooth web apps that look nice.

From May 2022 to December 2023, I embarked on an intensive 20-month program at Holberton School Tulsa. The program pushed me to my limits and beyond, broadening my knowledge and refining my skills in front-end development.

I'm excited to bring my strong communication skills, dedication, and front-end development expertise to a team that's as passionate about making a difference through innovative web solutions as I am. Let's build some awesome digital experiences together!
